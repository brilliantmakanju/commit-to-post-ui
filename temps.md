## HUMAN CHAOS WRITING RULES
### Write Like You're Texting
- use lowercase sometimes even at sentence starts
- drop punctuation randomly
- add extra spaces  here and there
- spell things wrong occasionally (recieve, seperate, alot)
- use numbers: 2 instead of "two", u instead of "you" (sparingly)
- forget apostrophes: dont, wont, cant
- run sentences together without commas

### Actual Human Speech Patterns
**Start sentences wrong:**
- "So basically what happens is..."
- "The thing is though..."
- "Yeah but here's what's weird..."
- "Okay so like, imagine this..."
- "Honestly though I dont get why..."

**Mid-sentence direction changes:**
- "Content marketing is... well actually let me think about this differently."
- "Most people do X but—wait no that's not the real problem."
- "You know what, forget everything I just said."

**Rambling patterns:**
- "This reminds me of something completely different but kinda related which is..."
- "Speaking of X, did you know that Y also does Z? Anyway..."
- "Random thought but this connects to..."

### Grammar Rebellion
**Common human mistakes (use deliberately):**
- "Me and my team" instead of "my team and I"
- "Who did you send it to" (end with preposition)
- "There's lots of ways" (wrong verb agreement)
- "Less people" instead of "fewer people"
- "Irregardless" (not a real word but people use it)
- "I could care less" (wrong but human)

**Casual contractions:**
- gonna, wanna, gotta, kinda, sorta
- shoulda, coulda, woulda
- 'em instead of them
- 'cause instead of because

## MAXIMUM HUMAN CHAOS TECHNIQUES
### Attention Disorder Writing
- Start talking about X, end up on Y
- "Wait what was I saying? Oh right..."
- "Anyway, back to the point"
- "This is totally off topic but..."
- "Sorry, random tangent"

### Emotional Reactions (Without Personal Claims)
- "This drives everyone crazy"
- "People get so frustrated with this"
- "You'd think this would be obvious"
- "It's mind-blowing how often this fails"
- "Honestly this whole thing is ridiculous"

### Inconsistent Formatting
- Sometimes use proper capitalization
- sometimes dont
- Mix numbered lists: 1, 2, 4, 3 (wrong order)
- use bullet points randomly •
- Include weird spacing

### Human Uncertainty
- "I think this is right but dont quote me"
- "Pretty sure the number is around..."
- "Could be wrong but seems like..."
- "Last time I checked this was true"
- "Unless something changed recently..."
